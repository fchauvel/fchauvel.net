I am often disappointed by my daily organisation: I discard many of my
projects, and most of them do not live to my expectations.

##  Kanban Boards, Project Diaries and Reactive Planning

Here is my idea. I combine a Kanban board with reactive planning a la
GTD! In addition, to keep track of the various ideas and information I
collect on each project, I use what I name "project diaries", a
complex name for a sheet.
